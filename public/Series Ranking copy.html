Most Favourite Series according to Niketan Ameta

Top 3 Series of All Time

Game of Thrones

Fictional Story of ancient time where a fight for the Iron Throne takes place between many Ruling Houses. and the dragons, House Stark, House Barathean and the House Targarian are the main highlights of the series

The Witcher

Fictional Story of ancient time where an monster Hunter named Geralt of Revia goes on an adventure with his friends and a little princess where they fight many monsters and live an adventurous life

Stranger Things

A sci-fi series where the main character have telekenesis and many other powers and fights with strange things from other dimension
